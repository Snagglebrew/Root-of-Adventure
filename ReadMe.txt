Instructions:
- Move around and jump with the arrow keys or WASD
- Attack with space or Z (For now its fire but will have customizable options later)
- Interact with chest and doors with E
- Dodge and defeat enemies
- Collect amber off the tree for trade
- move through rooms of different choices (In sprites the different types are listed)
